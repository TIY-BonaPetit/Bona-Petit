About Bona Petite!

Bona Petite is the leader in arduino board farming techniques!

The hardware setup runs on a special subset of C/C++ which is designed for use with Arduino boards. We also have a RaspberryPi board working as a communication device running a python reader which interprets the data from the Arduino and sends it to our Heroku built page.
